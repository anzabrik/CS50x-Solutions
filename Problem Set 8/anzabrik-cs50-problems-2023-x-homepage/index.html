Hi! My name is Sceptic Hmy, and I'm a biologist, philosopher, and avid hiker.

Early life

I was born circa 1780 in the family of I.Y. and H.B., who are accomplished professionals and simply great creatures. Taking to consideration the nature of my parents, I find it difficult to put it into words what they look like and who they are. Suffice it to say, my mom, I.Y., has large and soft ears, much like a koala (although it would be ridiculously inaccurate to describe her as an actual koala). My dad, H.B., in his turn, has several antennae on his head. They tend to forget the exact number of their kids, let alone their names, which is forgivable, taking into consideration I have over 112 siblings. Some of them are unbelievably nice.
